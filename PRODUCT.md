# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Multi-segment Indonesian market: UMKM/SME owners seeking digital transformation, government entities (villages, schools, mosques) needing systems and training, companies of various scales, finance institutions, and individuals seeking tech training. All share a common context: they need a trustworthy technology partner, not just a vendor. Visitors typically arrive via search ("jasa pembuatan website Bogor", "software house Indonesia") and make fast decisions about credibility.

## Product Purpose

InspiraLabs (Nawa Inspira Digital) is a software house and IT agency based in Bogor, Indonesia. The site serves as the primary trust-building surface: it must convert multi-segment audiences into consultation requests (WhatsApp CTA, contact form, survey) by demonstrating expertise across four pillars — Software Development, IoT & Robotik, Desain & Branding, and Pelatihan Teknologi. Success = a prospect who was a stranger becomes a client who sends a WhatsApp message.

## Brand Personality

Mitra Solusi Digital — Trusted Partner, Solution-Oriented, Grounded. The brand feels like a reliable expert colleague who knows Indonesia's real business challenges: not a faceless vendor, but a partner who understands the terrain. Professional enough for enterprise, approachable enough for a first-time business owner.

## Anti-references

- Generic IT agency template: dark/navy gradient hero, stock-photo team shots, identical feature cards with icons, "We deliver solutions" copy. No hero metrics grid.
- SaaS-cream aesthetic: warm-neutral body background, rounded glass cards, big number stats, eyebrows on every section. The cream background (`#F5EFE6`) already in the token set is a risk — use it sparingly as a surface accent, not as the primary body background.
- AI design slop in general: tracked all-caps eyebrows above every section, gradient text, numbered section markers (01/02/03), side-stripe borders, identical card grids.

## Design Principles

1. **Trust through substance** — Show real portfolio, real client names, real delivered systems. No filler copy. Every section must earn its place with specific evidence.
2. **Partner energy, not vendor energy** — Warm, direct, Indonesian-first language. Copy speaks like a capable colleague, not a pitch deck.
3. **Breadth without blur** — Four service pillars must be instantly scannable, but the visitor should never feel lost. One clear primary CTA per screen.
4. **Identity over template** — Maroon-gold palette is the brand signal; deploy it boldly rather than diluting it into neutral beige. Committed brand colors are an asset.
5. **Degrade gracefully** — Primary users are on mid-range Android devices over Indonesian mobile data. Motion must respect `prefers-reduced-motion`; all UI must be functional without JavaScript animations.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA baseline: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, large text ≥3:1, keyboard navigable, focus styles visible. Reduced motion support required — many users on lower-end Android devices. No color-only status cues.
